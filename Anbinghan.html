About

Binghan An is a master's student at the School of Information Science and Engineering, Shandong Normal University, with a research focus on reinforcement learning.

Participated in the project 'Data-based Output Synchronization of Discrete-Time Heterogeneous Multiagent Systems with Sensor Faults' under the supervision of my advisor. This project investigated output synchronization in heterogeneous multi-agent systems with sensor faults, proposing a data-based detection mechanism and a data-based FTC (Fault-Tolerant Control) approach.
